## Root Cause Analysis

### ANNOT-01..03 Regression

The v0.25-02 SUMMARY files and VALIDATION.md confirmed "0 unannotated properties" at completion time. However, the `qgsd-spec-regen.js` PostToolUse hook regenerates generated model files (QGSDQuorum.tla, QGSDQuorum_xstate.tla, quorum-votes.als, quorum.props) on certain triggers, stripping manually-added annotations.

**Affected files (15 properties):**
- `.formal/tla/QGSDQuorum.tla` — 7 properties (TypeOK, UnanimityMet, QuorumCeilingMet, AllTransitionsValid, DeliberationBounded, DeliberationMonotone, EventualConsensus)
- `.formal/tla/QGSDQuorum_xstate.tla` — 1 property (TypeOK)
- `.formal/alloy/quorum-votes.als` — 3 assertions (ThresholdPasses, BelowThresholdFails, ZeroApprovalsFail)
- `.formal/prism/quorum.props` — 4 properties (P=?/R=? queries)

**Resolution requires:** Either annotation-aware regeneration in spec-regen.js, or annotation injection as a post-regeneration step.
