### OBS-01/02/03/04 — mcp-status.md Regression (Phase 26)

The v2 `commands/qgsd/mcp-status.md` (125 lines, 10-agent, scoreboard-aware) was committed at `29a8236` (19:05). It was subsequently overwritten with old v1 (103 lines, 4-agent, provider-probe-based) at 19:48 during Phase 28 work.

`git status` confirms: `M commands/qgsd/mcp-status.md` (modified, unstaged).

**v1 behavior (live today):**
- Shows only 4 agents: codex-cli, gemini-cli, opencode, copilot-cli
- Does not poll claude-deepseek, claude-minimax, claude-qwen-coder, claude-kimi, claude-llama4, claude-glm
- Does not read `rounds[].votes` from scoreboard
- Does not derive health state (available/quota-exceeded/error)
- Does not show `available_models` column
- `allowed-tools` frontmatter has only `Bash` — no identity tools

**Fix (single command):** `git checkout -- commands/qgsd/mcp-status.md && cp commands/qgsd/mcp-status.md ~/.claude/commands/qgsd/mcp-status.md`

### STD-10 (Partial) — gemini ~/.claude.json Not Updated
