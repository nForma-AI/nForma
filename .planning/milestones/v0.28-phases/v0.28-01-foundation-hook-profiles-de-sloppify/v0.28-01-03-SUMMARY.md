## Decisions Made
- Profile guard placed AFTER stdin consumption to prevent deadlock -- the guard is inside the `on('end')` callback, not before it
- Strict mode uses a single broadened regex instead of modifying quorum_commands list -- cleaner and avoids mutating shared config
- nf-circuit-breaker.js and nf-precompact.js intentionally not modified -- they are always-active hooks per HOOK_PROFILE_MAP (MonitoringReachable invariant)
