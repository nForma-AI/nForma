## Pre-conditions for Phase 40

The integration checker identified structural obstacles for Phase 40 that exceed a simple "add quorum.active" implementation:
1. `config-loader.js`, `qgsd-prompt.js`, `templates/qgsd.json`, and the orchestrator all need `quorum.active` wiring simultaneously — no partial path exists
2. `update-scoreboard.cjs` requires a schema redesign for slot-based tracking (VALID_MODELS is model-family keyed); Phase 40 must treat this as a schema migration, not just an addition
3. `check-provider-health.cjs` needs a new dependency on `config-loader` or a CLI filter mechanism

These are planning considerations for Phase 40 research, not blockers for starting the phase.
