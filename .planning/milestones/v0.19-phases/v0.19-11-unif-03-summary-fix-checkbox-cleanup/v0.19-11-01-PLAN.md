<objective>
Fix the UNIF-03 timing bug: `run-formal-verify.cjs` triage summary reads `check-results.ndjson` before two CI enforcement scripts (`check-trace-redaction.cjs`, `check-trace-schema-drift.cjs`) have written their entries. Fix by adding both scripts as `STEPS` entries inside the orchestrator (tool: 'ci') so their writes happen before the NDJSON summary read inside `runOnce()`.

Purpose: The orchestrator summary currently reports N-2 NDJSON entries compared to `check-results-exit.cjs` (which runs last in CI and sees all entries). This makes the triage output misleading and breaks UNIF-03 compliance.

Output: Updated `bin/run-formal-verify.cjs` with 2 new CI STEPS entries + updated header comment (21→23). New UNIF-03 test in `bin/run-formal-verify.test.cjs` that first fails (RED) then passes (GREEN) after fix.
</objective>
