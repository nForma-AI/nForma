## Decisions Made

1. **Session ID fallback strategy**: CLAUDE_SESSION_ID from environment (injected by Claude Code) OR 'session-' + Date.now() if not available. Fallback ensures telemetry always has a session ID even in CI/local testing.

2. **Telemetry record shape**: 10 fields required (ts, session_id, round, slot, verdict, latency_ms, provider, provider_status, retry_count, error_type). Matches research specification exactly.

3. **Flakiness scoring window**: Exactly 10 rounds, trailing window (last 10 verdicts), not first 10. Ensures recent reliability matters more than historical average.

4. **Dispatch ordering**: Two-level sort — flakiness ascending (primary, most reliable first), success rate descending (tiebreaker). Defaults: flakiness 0.0, success rate 0.5.

5. **Fail-open on all test file reads**: If call-quorum-slot.cjs, update-scoreboard.cjs, or qgsd-prompt.js cannot be read, test continues and lets the structural assertion fail with clear message — no test runner crashes.
