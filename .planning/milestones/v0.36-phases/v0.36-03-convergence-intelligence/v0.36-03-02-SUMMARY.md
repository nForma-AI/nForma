## Decisions Made
- Drift threshold: 10% relative change for non-zero baselines, absolute >2 for zero baselines (avoids division by zero while still catching meaningful drift)
- capped_layers only populated when max-3 cap is actually reached, not for partial dispatches below cap
- baseline_drift defaults to {detected:false} in nf-solve.cjs so the field always appears in --report-only output even before the report sub-skill runs
