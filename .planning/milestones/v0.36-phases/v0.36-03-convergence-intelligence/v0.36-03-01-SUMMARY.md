## Decisions Made
- CycleDetector uses sliding 4-point window: values[i]===values[i-2] AND values[i+1]===values[i-1] for any window starting at index 2+
- Bucket assignments: automatable includes forward + layer-alignment layers (9), manual includes D->C + reverse layers (4), informational includes evidence/maturity layers (6)
- Convergence report backward-compatible: pre-CONV-02 trend entries without buckets field show "not yet tracked" message
