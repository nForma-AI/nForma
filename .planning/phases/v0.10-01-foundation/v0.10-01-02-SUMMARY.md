## Decisions Made

- Used `hasKeyFn = (_) => true` inside listAgents() scope: `key_status` is only written after a health probe (editAgent/checkAgentHealth path), which only runs when a key is configured. If `key_status.status === 'invalid'` exists in qgsd.json, a key was definitely configured at probe time — badge fires unconditionally without needing secretsLib.
- Scoreboard loaded from `process.cwd()/.planning/quorum-scoreboard.json` (project-relative, not home-relative) — scoreboard is per-project artifact.
- No badge truncation for v0.10-01: `(name + keyBadge).padEnd(W.slot)` overflows the column when name + badge exceeds W.slot (14 chars). Acceptable for this milestone; typical slot names are 8-10 chars.
