# Plan 01 Summary: Layer Manifest and Event Vocabulary Foundation

- 15 spec modules have invariants.md files
- Generated `.planning/formal/layer-manifest.json` with layer arrays, gate relationships (A: L1->L2, B: L2->L3, C: L3->TC), and summary
- Extended `.planning/formal/model-registry.json` with source_layer, gate_maturity ("ADVISORY"), layer_maturity per model
- Created `bin/build-layer-manifest.test.cjs` — 9 tests (classification rules + integration)
