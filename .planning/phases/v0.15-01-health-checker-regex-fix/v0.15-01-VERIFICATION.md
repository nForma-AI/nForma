### Deviation Note

The SUMMARY documents one substantive deviation from the PLAN: the phasePattern at lines 3613 and 3863 was extended with a checkbox-format alternation `(?:#{2,4}|[-*]\s+\[[^\]]\]\s+\*{1,2})` beyond what the plan specified. This was required because phases v0.8-01 and v0.11-01 use `- [x] **Phase v0.X-YY:**` format in ROADMAP.md (not `### Phase v0.X-YY:`). Without this fix, two new W007 regressions would have appeared after the disk extractor fix. The deviation increases scope correctly and is covered by the test suite.
