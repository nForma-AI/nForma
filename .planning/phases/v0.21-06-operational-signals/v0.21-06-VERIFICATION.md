**Status: SATISFIED**

- Runs in under 10ms (performance test assertion)
- 10/10 tests GREEN

**Evidence:**

- `checkConsensusGate()` with priors returns `proceed` (P ~ 0.988 >> 0.70 threshold)
- `checkConsensusGate()` with threshold=0.99 and minQuorum=3 correctly returns `defer`
