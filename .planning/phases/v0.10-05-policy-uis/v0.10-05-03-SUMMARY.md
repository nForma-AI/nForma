- `writeUpdatePolicy()` persists to `qgsd.json` under `agent_config[slot].update_policy` (NOT `providers.json`)
- `tuneTimeouts()` writes to `providers.json` via `writeProvidersJson()` — the correct target per PLCY-01 spec
