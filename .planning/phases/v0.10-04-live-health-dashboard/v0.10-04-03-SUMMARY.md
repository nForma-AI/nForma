### Non-TTY Bug Fix (commit 7c55184)

**Bug discovered:** `node bin/manage-agents.cjs | cat` crashed with `ERR_USE_AFTER_CLOSE` because `mainMenu()` ran inquirer before `liveDashboard()` could reach its `!isTTY` guard.

**Fix:** Entry point now routes non-TTY directly to `liveDashboard()`:
```javascript
const entry = process.stdout.isTTY ? mainMenu : liveDashboard;
entry().catch(...)
```
