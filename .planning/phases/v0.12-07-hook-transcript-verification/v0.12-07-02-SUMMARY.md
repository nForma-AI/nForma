# Plan 02 Summary — Alloy Spec (transcript-scan.als)

## What Was Built

- **formal/alloy/transcript-scan.als** — Alloy 6 formal spec for qgsd-stop.js transcript scanning algorithm

## Spec Structure

- `module transcript_scan`
- Sig hierarchy: `Entry` (abstract, `idx: one Int`) → `HumanMessage`, `ToolUse`, `ToolResult`
- `Config` one sig with `minSize: one Int`
- Facts: `UniqueIndices`, `ContiguousIndices` (integer ordering, not Alloy seq)
- Funs: `boundaryIdx`, `successfulResults`, `successCount`
- Preds: `BoundaryCorrect`, `PairingUnique`, `NoDuplicateCounting`, `SuccessCountNeverExceedsMinSize`
- 4 assert+check pairs, all scoped `for 5 Entry, 5 Id, 2 Bool, 4 Int`

## Key Design Decisions

1. `lone` quantifier in PairingUnique — "at most one" (not `one`) avoids Pitfall 2
2. Integer `idx` field — avoids Alloy seq scope limitation (Pitfall 1)
3. `SuccessCountNeverExceedsMinSize` name — upper-bound semantics, references qgsd-stop.js:453 break
4. 8 lines from `grep "^assert\|^check"` — confirmed 4 asserts + 4 checks
5. 4 check commands with `for 5 Entry, 5 Id, 2 Bool, 4 Int` scope confirmed
