## Requirements Coverage

### DECOMP-01: State-Space Risk Analysis
**Status:** PASSED

- `analyze-state-space.cjs --json` produces per-model risk classification for all 22 TLA+ models
- Risk distribution: HIGH=12, MODERATE=5, LOW=0, MINIMAL=5
- Each model entry includes: module_name, cfg_file, variables (with domain/cardinality/bounded), constants, estimated_states, risk_level, risk_reason, invariant_count, property_count

### DECOMP-02: Unbounded Domain Flagging
**Status:** PASSED

- 12 models flagged with `has_unbounded: true` and populated `unbounded_domains` arrays
- Unbounded domains identified across different types:
  - Set unions: `AccountIds \union {NoAccount}` (QGSDAccountManager)
  - Unknown domains: `slotStatus: unknown`, `callState: unknown` (QGSDMCPEnv)
  - Sequences: `commits: Seq(Labels)`, `runs: Seq(Labels)` (QGSDOscillation)
  - Naturals: `slotsAvailable: Nat`, `successCount: Nat` (QGSDQuorum_xstate)
  - Power sets: `recruited: SUBSET AllSlots` (QGSDRecruiting)
- Risk reason explicitly states unbounded domain for each HIGH model
